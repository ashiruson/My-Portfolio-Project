About SistersGlam

SistersGlam Enterprise is a fashion designing and tailoring company registered by Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in January 2021 with business number (BN): 4032058. The company is located at No. AG 9 Giwa Road Tudun Wada Kaduna.

At SistersGlam, we provide tailoring services by making and selling casual wears, birthday wears, wedding gowns for bridal showers, ready to wear gowns, kimono, palazzo pants and trousers, tops and skirts, turban caps, Hijabs and jibabs.....and lots more.
